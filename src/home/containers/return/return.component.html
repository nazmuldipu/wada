Return & Refund Policy

Make changes or Cancelling Your Online Order and Refund Process
  1. Make change your order: you can cancel or amend your order at any time up to 11pm the day before your order is due to be delivered. We allow up to five amendments per order if the order value is increased during an order change. If the order value remains the same value or less, there are no limitations on how many times you can amend the order. All increases in order value are subject to the pre-authorization check on your payment account.
  2. Cancelling your order: You also have a legal right to cancel an order at any time up to 14 working days (not including Friday, Saturday or public holidays) from the day after the day the products are delivered to you. You are welcome to change your mind and decide that you do not want to keep a product. You can notify us of your decision to cancel and receive a refund. This right to cancel does not apply to:
  3. Perishable products, such as fresh food or drink; and/or perishable products, such as fresh food or drink; and/or
  4. Term which cannot be resold for health and hygiene reasons once unwrapped (for example, personal grooming products and certain baby products etc.)
  5. How to cancel:
    Standard delivery (including next day delivery slots)
    • To cancel an order before 11pm: Please either log in to your online account, select order history, and cancel the relevant order, or contact us via our Customer Care team. You will need to provide the email address registered with your account and confirm the order number you wish to cancel. The pre-authorization on the card will be cancelled this may take up to 5 working days to process depending on your issued date.
    • To cancel an order after 11pm: Please contact our Customer Care team, by filling in this online form or call Customer Care on: 01730785675-9 and provide the email address registered with your account and confirm the order number you wish to cancel. The money will be refunded to your account as soon as possible and, in any case, within 14 calendar days of notification of cancellation.
  6. Same day delivery:
    To cancel a same day order before 12pm
  7. Simply log into your account, and go to Order History, and select the Cancel button in the Current Order section. The money will be refunded to your account as soon as possible and, in any case, within 14 calendar days of notification of cancellation.
  8. To cancel an order once the goods are received: Please contact our Customer Care team by filling in this online form or call Customer Care on: 01730785675-9 and provide the email address registered with your account number. We will process the refund as soon as possible and, in any case, within 14 calendar days after the day on which we receive the products back, or 14 days after the day on which you gave us the evidence of having sent the products back, whichever is sooner. We will usually refund you by the same method as your original payment.
  9. Reasonable care of goods: You must take reasonable care of the products to be returned and store them in an appropriate manner, e.g. frozen products or Products that have a short shelf life must be kept in a freezer/suitable temperature. Unless the products were misdescribed on our website or are faulty, we may require you to return the products at your own cost. The products must be returned undamaged and in the original packaging.
